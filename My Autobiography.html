My Autobiography

About Me

Hello! My name is Mary Lis B. Pacaldo, and I was born on August 11 2007. I am currently 18 years old. I come from a loving and supportive family that has always been my greatest source of strength. We are six in the family, my parents, my three sisters, and me. Being the youngest among my siblings has taught me a lot about responsibility, patience, and teamwork. My sisters and I are very close, we share everything, from laughter to late-night talks. Even though we sometimes argue, our bond always grows stronger after every misunderstanding. My parents have always guided us with love and wisdom, reminding us to stay humble and kind. At home, we enjoy spending time together, especially during weekends and special occasions. I am truly grateful to have a family that supports me in everything I do. As a student, I take my studies seriously because I want to make my family proud.

Early Life and Education

I spent my childhood in Cordova, where I spent most of my early years surrounded by my loving family. My early life was filled with happy memories of playing with friends and discovering new things each day. As a child, I was naturally curious and eager to learn about the world around me. I started my education at Day-as Elementary School, where I first learned the importance of hard work and discipline. My teachers were kind and patient, and they inspired me to always do my best in everything. And when I moved on to high school, I became more focused on my studies and personal goals. High school life was full of challenges, but it also gave me opportunities to grow and discover my strengths. I made wonderful friends and learned the value of teamwork and perseverance.There were times when I faced difficulties, but with the support of my family and teachers, I never gave up. After graduating from high school, I decided to continue my journey by entering college. I am now studying at Lapu-Lapu City College, taking up BIT COMPTECH. College life is very different from high school, but it teaches me independence and responsibility. I've learned how to manage my time, balance my studies, and handle pressure more effectively. Every day in college is a new opportunity to gain knowledge and improve myself. I have met new friends who share the same goals and dreams, which makes my college experience even more meaningful. So, I am grateful for every experience that has shaped who I am today, a determined and hopeful college student striving to achieve my dreams.

Achievements and Goals

Throughout my life, I have always been motivated to achieve my best in everything I do. One of my proudest achievements was graduating high school with honors, which reflected my hard work and dedication. I also participated in several academic competitions, where I earned awards that boosted my confidence. In the future, I hope to choose a college course that suits my interests and strengths. I want to develop skills that will help me get a good job someday. I dream of becoming someone who can support my family and live comfortably. To reach these goals, I know I need to study regularly and stay focused. I plan to manage my time better so I can balance schoolwork and hobbies. Though I face challenges sometimes, I try not to give up and keep moving forward. I believe that with patience and effort, I can achieve my dreams step by step. And my goal is to stay healthy by exercising and eating well so I have energy to study. I hope to volunteer or help in my community to make a positive difference. I want to become more confident in myself and my abilities. My biggest goal is to never give up, even when things get hard. I believe that with hard work and determination, I can reach all my goals and have a bright future.

My Personality and Motto

I would describe myself as I am a hardworking and curious individual who enjoys learning new things every day. I believe that knowledge is the key to personal growth and success. I like to challenge myself because I know that growth comes from pushing beyond my comfort zone. People often say I am dependable and trustworthy, which I take pride in. I am also a good listener and enjoy helping others whenever I can. Being kind and respectful to everyone around me is very important to me. I value honesty and try to always be truthful in my words and actions. In difficult situations, I remain calm and try to find practical solutions. I understand that failure is part of learning, so I never fear making mistakes. My friends and family describe me as optimistic and supportive. I enjoy working both independently and as part of a team. Discipline and consistency are qualities I work hard to maintain. One thing I always remind myself is to stay humble no matter how much I achieve. My life motto is “Keep moving forward, no matter how slow the progress, because persistence leads to success.” This motto reminds me to be patient with myself and to never give up. I believe that with determination and hard work, anything is possible. So I strive to be a better person each day, learning from my experiences and helping those around me.".
